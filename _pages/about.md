<p style="text-align:justify">
I am a postgraduate student at the <b>University of Edinburgh</b> (Artificial intelligence MSc - School of Informatics). 
</p>

<p style="text-align:justify">
In 2019, I was awarded with a Diploma of Electrical and Computer Engineering, from the <b>University of Patras</b>, in Greece (graduated 4th in my class – GPA: 8.11 out of 10). 
</p>
  
<p style="text-align:justify">
As a student I conducted research for using deep CNNs in hand-gesture recognition via sEMG signals, which led to the development of a real-time hand-gesture recognition software for the Myo Armband (<a href="https://youtu.be/w98PkUeSu20">demo</a>) and the recording of the <a href="https://github.com/tsagkas/MyoUP_dataset">MyoUP sEMG dataset</a> (Supervised by <a href="http://www.ece.upatras.gr/skodras/">Prof. Athanassios Skodras</a>).
</p>

<p style="text-align:justify">
Moreover, working under the supervision of <a href="http://www.vvr.ece.upatras.gr/members/konstantinos-moustakas/">Prof. Konstantinos Moustakas</a> and the <a href="http://www.vvr.ece.upatras.gr/">VVR group</a>, I studied the use of Haptics in Human-Computer Interaction, by taking part in the <a href="http://www.worldhaptics2019.org/program/sicmobil">2nd Student Challenge in Automotive Haptics</a>, at the <a href="http://www.worldhaptics2019.org/">World Haptics Conference</a>, Tokyo, Japan, in 2019, where our team received the  <a href="http://www.vvr.ece.upatras.gr/news/announcements-158-ieee-worldhaptics-2019-tokyo-best-student-innovation-challenge-award/">Best Student Innovation Challenge Award</a>.
</p>

<p style="text-align:justify">
Currently, I am a member of the <a href="https://eufs.eusa.ed.ac.uk/ai">UEFS</a> - AI Perception team, solving Computer Vision related problems in an autonomous racecar. Until June 2020, I worked as a Data Scientist at <a href="https://www.ey.com/en_gr">Ernst & Young</a>, where I participated in projects of the shipping, tobacco and betting industry. Before that I worked as a Laboratory Assistant at the <a href="http://dsip.ece.upatras.gr/">DSIP</a> lab, at the University of Patras.  
</p>
